## Kd Ocean Colour Blue by Sentinel-5P TROPOMI (Daily)
The Kd Ocean Colour (blue) product provides the diffuse attenuation coefficient (Kd) in the short blue wavelength range (390–423 nm), quantifying **how sunlight is absorbed and scattered in the upper ocean layer**.

Data are essential for monitoring ocean optical properties, phytoplankton, and marine ecosystem health.

#### About the dataset
- Kd-blue measures the attenuation of downwelling plane irradiance in the ocean’s first optical depth.
- The product is generated using Differential Optical Absorption Spectroscopy (DOAS) adapted to oceanic applications (PhytoDOAS), with retrievals converted into physical Kd values using radiative transfer look-up tables. Only high-quality measurements (qa_value == 1.0) are used in the final gridded product.

#### Use cases
Kd-blue measurements are particularly useful for:
- Studying light penetration and ocean color dynamics.
- Ocean biogeochemistry and primary productivity modeling.
- Monitoring phytoplankton blooms and water quality.
- Climate studies related to ocean radiative transfer and carbon cycling.
